Bombay Market

The problem

Bombay Market had been around since 2017, but was not well represented on the internet. The former Bombay Market website was created by the previous owner of the shop and it contained lots of outdated information, and the new owners had no way to take this website down as they lacked access to it.

Our solution

After meeting with the new owners of the shop, we wanted to give Bombay Market a stronger online presence. We did this by taking the old website off of the internet and creating a brand new website to highlight their goods and present information that they want their customers to know.

The problem

Champaign Central FBLA’s chapter has been growing since it was established in 2016. The only problem with the chapter was that it did not have a useful place to showcase information, links, and community involvement displayed in one place. Because both of us are active members and officers of this chapter, we offered to develop a website that would market, promote, and inform our community and members about FBLA events. The chapter agreed to use our services and we developed this website that has proven to be useful as we included quick access links and information.
